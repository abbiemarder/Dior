Christain Dior

A French fashion designer whose works dominated the decade immediately after World War II.

Early Life

Christain Ernest Dior, was born and raised in Normandy, France and moved to Paris with his parents when he was ten years old. He was one of seven children in his family and was the second born. His parents were Maurice and Madeleine in the town of Granville. Dior was always interested in art but his father insisted on a good education. This was where he enrolled atthe prestigious Ecole Des Sciences Politiques in Paris. He finished with degree in Politics which prepared him for the next step in his life.  

Mid Life

Dior always wished to be a diplomat.  

Late Life

Dior, was born and raised in Normandy, France and moved to Paris with his parents when he was ten years old. He joined the military after he had graduated in political science. The start of his design career came in 1935, when he returned to Paris and started selling sketches.
